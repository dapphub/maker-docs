A "unit of account" is an asset whose PriceStability is such that it can be used to measure and compare other prices over time. A FiatCurrency is typically the unit of account for most economic activity occuring within its corresponding jurisdiction. While a CryptoCurrency typically makes for a poor unit of account (due to lack of price stability), StableCoins exist to solve this problem. See also MediumOfExchange, StoreOfValue.
