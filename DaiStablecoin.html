The "dai" is a StableCoin autonomously managed by the DaiCreditSystem. It is designed to maintain PriceStability in the ShortTerm (making it useful as a UnitOfAccount and a MediumOfExchange), and to tend towards PriceDeflation in the LongTerm (making it a potential StoreOfValue). The StabilityAnchor of the dai is the SpecialDrawingRight (SDR).
