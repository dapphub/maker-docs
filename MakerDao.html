MakerDAO (or "Maker") is a DecentralizedAutonomousOrganization that backs the value of the DaiStablecoin (DAI) on the EthereumBlockchain through deployment and continued maintenance of the DaiCreditSystem. In return for for performing SystemGovernance and for taking on the risk of BailOuts, Maker earns a StabilityFee on all outstanding dai. This income is funnelled directly to owners of the MkrToken (MKR).
